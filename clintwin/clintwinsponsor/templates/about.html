About Us:

The ClinTwin360 is a Software as a Service (SaaS) firm based out of Cambridge, MA that offers a powerful digital platform to help sponsor organizations revolutionize the clinical trials design, participants engagement and trial analytics. To improve the clinical trial process, ClinTwin360 leverages its digital platform for sourcing participants by enabling a more sponsor-focused recruitment procedure, improves the awareness of clinical trials while also enriching clinical trial data with patient health data from wearables and the providing the ability to analyze the data to support continuous improvement of clinical trials.
