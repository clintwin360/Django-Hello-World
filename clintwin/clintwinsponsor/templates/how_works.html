How Does It Work?

The major benefits of ClinTwin360 include:
1. Reduced enrollment inefficiencies with a centralized and robust clinical trial matching service that is exposed to a larger participant pool of mobile phone users
2. Enriches clinical trial data with participant biometrics that provide insights and improve trial effectiveness
3. Optimizes clinical trial execution through analysis of a wide range of clinical trial and biometric data
